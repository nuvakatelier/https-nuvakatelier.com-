The process

Slow. Intentional. Irrepetible.

01

The line is drawn

Each design is composed with intention before it touches the fabric.

02

The fabric is prepared

The drawing is placed, the space is considered and the rhythm is set.

03

The embroidery is stitched

Every stitch is made by hand. Slowly. Carefully. One line at a time.

04

The piece is released once

Once finished, it becomes one of a kind. One piece. One owner.

The maker

Designed by an architect. Stitched by hand.

Architecture taught the studio to value proportion, silence and balance. Embroidery became another way to draw.

NUVAK is where both worlds meet: line, fabric, time and botanical traces.
